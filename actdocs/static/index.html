NPW 2014

Helsinki Perl Mongers invite you to Helsinki at the Nordic Perl Workshop 2014.

The Workshop consist of a hackathon on November 16th at Frantic offices in Helsinki city centre and the workshop and social event on November 17th at Restaurant Ship Wäiski in Hakaniemi, Helsinki.

We welcome you to register and submit your talks and to send us your ideas for the hackathon to npw2014@frantic.com.
